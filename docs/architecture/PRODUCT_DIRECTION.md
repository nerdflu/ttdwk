# Product Direction

## Not a Google Maps Clone
ThingsToDoWithKids.com.au is a discovery engine, not just a directory of coordinates.
We want to inspire parents with ideas, mini-adventures, hidden gems, and simple weekend plans rather than just listing every playground in existence.

## Mix of Content
By mixing Places, Events, Ideas, and Guides, we provide actionable value.
An "Idea" like "Rockpool hunting before lunch" is more valuable to a parent than just a pin on a map for "Burleigh Heads Rockpools".

## Operational Goal
Long-term, the platform should be heavily automated.
Bots and AI agents will scan trusted sources and emails to queue up content in Google Sheets.
A human editor simply approves or rejects rows, and the static site rebuilds daily.
